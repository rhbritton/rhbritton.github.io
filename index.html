PRICSM

Retirement Community Software

Overview:

Built with Nodejs, Express, Mongoose, Dust.js, Stylus, and React

Unit Tested with Mocha

Hosted with AWS EC2, Elastic Beanstalk, S3, Route 53, and CodePipeline.

Current Daily Users: ~300


Leases:

Tracks leases, past and future lease changes, resident information.


Billing:

Keeps a ledger for each lease. You can generate bills in bulk and send out invoices for residents.


Marketing:

Track interactions with potential residents and referrals. Generate reports that show which referrals and marketers are most efficient.


Resident Care:

Create care plans for residents and assign tasks on each shift that evenly distributes the work load based on estimated time to completion.


Maintenance:

Track and manage one off or recurring works orders.

Built to solve various retirement community problems based on customer feedback and needs.
